Software Onboarding: Google Account

General Information

You have a Google account within the Northvolt network with mail, calendar and cloud storage all in one.

Instructions

The first time you access your google account we need you to enable 2-Step Verification of your account, also known as two-factor authentication. We need you to do this to add an extra layer of security to our Northvolt network, allowing you to access the account with your password and a unique verification code that's sent to your phone.

Please follow the link: www.google.com/landing/2step/

Note: If you don’t activate this two-factor authentication of your account, you risk to be locked out from the Northvolt network.

Google Drive

We use Google Drive to share documents and files internally. All content residing on Northvolt shared drive is the property of the company and Northvolt users should store all document and files on drive as it automatically backs-up on Northvolt cloud. Northvolt users are encouraged to use Google’s desktop sync client and take precautions for synchronization to the cloud as this is the only backup setup within the Northvolt network.
