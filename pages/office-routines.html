Policys & Routines: Office Routines

General Information

The following is office space routines for the Gamla Brogatan 26 office.

Postal Adress

Gamla Borgatan 26, 3rd floor
11 20 Stockholm
Sweden

Entrance code: 9575

Sector Alarm

There are 2 locks with the same key. If the upper lock is locked, then the office is secured with an alarm.

Turn on the alarm: Only enter your personal 4-digit code
Turn off the alarm: Only enter your personal 4-digit code

If not successful, call Sector alarm and provide them with your name and your personal 4-digit code, see instructions on alarm gadget.

Make sure that you are the last person in the premise and that the door in the rightwing is locked. Close all windows and turn off the light. Turn on the alarm and make sure to lock both the upper and lower lock.

Note: The two doors close to the steps are constantly alarmed and are only used as emergency exits.

Conference rooms

There are 2 conference rooms, Stora konfen och Lilla konfen. You book the conference rooms in you Mac Calendar or Outlook calendar, see separate installations instructions.

Door Bell

To let visitors in in the main entrance at the ground floor answer the cell phone and press #5.

Opening hours entrance

The entrance code works Monday-Friday 06-19 all other hours one must the key card to access the building.

In case of emergency

There are 2 emergency exits in the premise.

One is located the corner of the open office landscape next to the steps and is accessible from the right wing and the centre body of the space.

The other one is located on the entry level, located on the left side of the steps and accessible from the conference rooms and co-working area.
